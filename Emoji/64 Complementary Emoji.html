Compatibility

These new emoji go beyond extant implementations.

Frequently requested

Of the few prominent public curated sources of requested emoji I did consult I could not find most of my own; so rather than repeat the work of limited scope of others I went my own route altogether for useful and playful everyday concepts still in want; I also made each of the sample images and release them under the CC0 licence.. It's not always the best idea to solicit a popular wish list; the less-popular features by luck may not get needed attention to then become as popular. Of my requests a consistent set of Google search terms, if necessary, narrows down the usage to the intended concept so the results score relate between the different classes of objects. With the properly-Japanese emoji for monsters stripped of their native name for the Western European equivalent, the same search yields at least a half million results each1,2, like most of my requests. As Google's indexers find pages that no longer exist, the subject disappears from attention and the results score drops.

Logos, brands, UI icons, signage, specific people, deities
